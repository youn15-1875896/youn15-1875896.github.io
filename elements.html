We’ve considered every detail and amenity with the aim of creating a living and social space that fosters connection, learning, and wellness—a housing experience that truly reflects the lifestyle and priorities of students today.

Studio apartments feature generous built-in storage and a study nook with ample space to organize your personal and academic collections. Kitchens include a full-size refrigerator, cooktop and built-in microwave oven.

The four-bedroom shared spaces are furnished and designed with comfort in mind. Fully equipped kitchens—including an oven, dishwasher, microwave and refrigerator—provide everything you need to have group dinners. Shared living spaces include expansive windows and hand-picked furniture to create the perfect student living atmosphere.

Relax, study and connect

Just across the courtyard you’ll find more than 2,300 square feet of communal space, designed to optimize study sessions and downtime. A dozen high-tech TVs with touch-screen controls are thoughtfully designed into the space, providing semi-private access for individual and group meetings.
